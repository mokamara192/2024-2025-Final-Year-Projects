Privacy and Policy

1. Introduction

At Wi Furniture Shop, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring that your personal information is handled securely and responsibly. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your data when you use our website.

2. Information We Collect

Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number, delivery address, and billing details when you create an account or place an order. Payment Information: Payment details for Stripe transactions (processed securely via Stripe; we do not store credit card details). Order Information: Details of products purchased, order history, and delivery preferences. Website Usage Data: IP address, browser type, device information, and interaction data through cookies and analytics tools.

3. How We Use Your Information

Process and fulfill orders, including payment transactions and deliveries. Communicate with you about your orders, account status, and promotions. Improve our website functionality and enhance user experience. Ensure secure transactions and protect against fraud.

4. Payment Security

We use Stripe as our online payment gateway, ensuring secure transactions through encryption and fraud protection measures. We do not store your payment details.
